Josh Dolgin

ON | CA

The Design and Engineering Report is a compilation of all my projects during high school, where you can see my development as an engineer and my dedication to problem solving and learning. The contents of the report span 3 years of course work. In grade 10 I was introduced to circuit theory, breadboarding, reading schematics, and through hole soldering. In grade 11 I learned Arduino C, Python, SMD soldering, vacuum forming, CAD (Tinkercad, Fusion 360, Onshape), and PCB Design (EAGLE). In grade 12 I moved on to assembly and machine language, built my own 4 bit computer, designed SMD PCB's, and continued to develop my skills in CAD and circuit design.

Some personal recommendations to check out are: (11) Smart Trash Can, (15) Bi-Wheeled Rover, (19) Chump, (20) Dolgin Development Platform, (23) SMD Desktop Equalizer, (26) Scrolling DER Page. Some of these sections may have independent writeups in my projects section.
